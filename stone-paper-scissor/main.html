Game Rules

  • Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock.
  • Agree ahead of time whether you will count off "rock,paper,scissor ,shoot" or just "rock,paper,scissor".
  • use rock,paper,scissor to settle for minor decision or simply play to pass the time.
